# Phase 4.5 Research Process Complexity Budget

## Objective
Prevent "full procedural armor on every experiment" while retaining rigor.

## Framework
### Discovery-Stage Process
- **Scope**: Fast, scoped, hypothesis-driven testing of single state families.
- **Mandatory Documentation**: `discovery_loop_registry.json` entry and short bullet points.
- **Gates**: No absolute gates. Go/no-go based entirely on conditional information gain and baseline beta safety.

### Governed-Comparison-Stage Process
- **Trigger**: Only invoked when a candidate incorporates `DISCOVERY_ACCEPTED` data, uses a supportable taxonomy, and passes identifiability audits.
- **Scope**: Full absolute gates, self-audit, and comparison against `phase3_two_stage_winner`.
- **Mandatory Documentation**: Full self-audit, comparison report, and checklist.

## Conclusion
This stratification prevents research from grinding to a halt on unviable data inputs, reserving the heavy governance machinery only for structurally viable candidates.
